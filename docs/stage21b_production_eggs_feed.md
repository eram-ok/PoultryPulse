# Stage 21B — Production, Egg Inventory, and Feed

Stage 21B replaces the production, egg inventory, and feed placeholders with authenticated operational workflows.

## Production
- paginated production records and summary metrics
- flock and status filtering
- draft creation and editing
- submit, confirm, and reject workflow
- confirmation posts graded eggs to inventory through the existing backend service

## Egg inventory
- current grade balances, trays, and loose eggs
- transaction ledger
- manual adjustments, internal issues, donations, damage, and reversals

## Feed
- inventory balance and low-stock visibility
- feed item registration
- purchase receipt
- flock usage
- adjustments and wastage

No backend source, migration, database schema, dependency, or environment change is required.
